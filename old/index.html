Who We Are

The Brown Chess Club is a club for any and every Brunonian interested in chess. We play chess, analyze games, compete in tournaments, and do all kinds of other fun stuff! Whether you're a complete novice or a seasoned pro, we'd love to help you on your journey to chess mastery. To get involved, you can use the button below or simply stop by one of our meetings!

Meeting Information

We currently meet weekly on Tuesdays from 8 PM to 10 PM. The location can vary from week to week, so either ask a member the day of the meeting or join our mailing list (using the button above) to know where the meeting is being held every time.
